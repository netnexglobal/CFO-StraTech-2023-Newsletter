Strategic Growth driver 1: CFO’s role in moving from past to future
In this panel on Data to Decisions : CFO's Guide to Agile Business, CFOs will discuss how as finance leaders, it's essential to make changes to people, leadership, and analytics in order to create enhanced reporting. To move forward dynamically, CFOs must be equipped with advanced analytics capabilities to unlock insights from data, and reboot the FP&A approach to enable more agile scenario planning. Similarly the data requires to be secured and used strategically to provide complete and timely actionable insights to deliver competitive advantage to the business.

Strategic Growth driver 2: CFO’s role in driving cost optimization
In this discussion on Leveraging costs with growth initiatives, CFOs will discuss the need to achieve a balance between operating margins and growth. Strategies for cost productivity enabling CFOs to handle inflationary pressures, while providing the resources to invest in initiatives that foster growth.
Strategic growth driver 3: CFO’s role in driving ESG
This session will talk about CFOs unique position to guide the ESG agenda of their respective organizations. With investors and stakeholders increasingly focusing on organizations’ sustainability goals, it is essential for CFOs to ensure that their organizations are well-placed to meet these expectations.
Strategic Growth driver 4: CFO’s role in transformation through Digital
In this panel on Cultivating Teams for the Digital Strategy, CFOs discuss how Tech-powered change is positioning finance functions to unlock greater value across organizations. The rise of advanced tools and automation isn’t poised to supplant workers but to supplement them. As these tools increase efficiency and productivity, finance functions will recover more time and resources with which their staff can pursue deeper, more critical tasks and How can the CFOs prepare their teams to help you realize value across your organization.
